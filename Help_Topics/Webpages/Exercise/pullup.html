Pullup

Beginner

How to Do a Basic Pullup:

  1. Hang from a bar with an overhand grip, arms fully extended, and feet off the ground.
  2. Pull yourself up by engaging your back and arms until your chin is above the bar.
  3. Lower yourself slowly back to the starting position with control.

Benefits:

  • Builds Upper-Body Strength
  • Improves Grip Strength
  • Supports Better Posture
  • Encourages Hormone Balance
